NODE: UCLA (3403)

ESTABLISHED: 1969-09-02 TYPE: University MACHINE: SDS Sigma 7 PI: L. Kleinrock
The first Interface Message Processor (IMP) was installed here. This node was the birthplace of the internet, sending the first message "LO" (intended to be LOGIN) to SRI in Stanford.
Historical Context

1969: The Cold War creates pressure for decentralized command and control. The Advanced Research Projects Agency (ARPA) funds the first 4 nodes.
